Mobile Homes Population at county level in Maryland, 2020

Inter-Provincial Migration and Economic Disparities in China, 2010

The main inter-provincial flows happen between neighboring provinces. Only a few provinces, such as Shanxi, Beijing, Shanghai and Anhui. These are also some of the places that attract the most flows and has most inflow population. Places that send the most flows are Shandong and Ningxia. The relationship between inflow population and economic disparities varies in different geographical divisions

Colorectal cancer, nitrate pollution, and population in Iowa census tracts

The maps of relative risk of colorectal cancer and nitrate concentration show high association of high values at the west, south east and east parts of Iowa. The map of population with high housing cost burden displays some tracts with values cluster at the south east and east regions, which indicates the association with these parts of the high colorectal cancer risk and nitrate concentration. The Hispanics populations center at the north west areas showing a similar pattern as the colorectal cancer risk and nitrate pollution in these areas.
